FAQ

Quick answers, zero fluff.

Is BroDex for beginners?

Yes. It can explain like you’re new or go deep when you want more.

Will it just do my homework?

It can, but the magic is learning. Ask for hints and explanations.

What should I paste when I’m stuck?

Your goal, your code, the exact error, and what you already tried.

What languages can it help with?

Java, Python, JavaScript, C/C++, and more.
