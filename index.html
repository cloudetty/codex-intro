FAQ

Quick answers.

Is BroDex for beginners?

Yes. It can explain like you’re new or go deep if you want the full story.

Will it just do my homework?

It can, but the magic is learning. Ask for hints and explanations, not just final code.

What should I paste when I’m stuck?

Your goal, your code, the exact error, and what you already tried.

What languages can it help with?

The common ones: Java, Python, JavaScript, C/C++, and more.
